    Cookie Decorating Party

    Ages 4+ - 20 minutes - Unlimited creativity

    Transform Cookies into Edible Art!

    Now that we've baked our sugar cookies, it's time for the really fun part - decorating! Learn simple icing techniques and create your own cookie masterpieces. This is where creativity meets deliciousness.

    Simple Icing Recipes:

    Basic Royal Icing:

    • 2 cups powdered sugar
    • 1 1/2 tablespoons meringue powder
    • 1/4 cup warm water

    Easy Buttercream Icing:

    • 1/2 cup softened butter
    • 2 cups powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla
    • 2 tablespoons milk

    Decorating Techniques:

    1. Make your chosen icing and divide into bowls
    2. Add food coloring to create different colors
    3. Use spoons or piping bags to apply icing
    4. Add sprinkles, candies, or edible glitter
    5. Let icing set before serving

    Creative Possibilities:

    • 3 Easy icing recipes for different effects
    • Sprinkles and decorations galore!
    • Perfect for playdates - share the fun

    Decorating Tips:

    • Use zip-top bags with corner cut for easy piping
    • Set up a "decorating station" with all supplies
    • Embrace the mess - it's part of the fun!
    • Take photos of your edible artwork
