    Easy Peasy Sugar Cookies

    Ages 5+ - 45 minutes - Cookie cutters fun

    Cookie Cutting Fun!

    Learn to make classic sugar cookies that are perfect for decorating. We'll practice rolling, cutting, and the magic of the oven! This recipe builds on the skills learned in our brownie adventure.

    Cookie Ingredients:

    • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1 cup softened butter
    • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
    • 1 egg
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Cookie Making Steps:

    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
    2. Mix flour, baking soda, and baking powder in a bowl
    3. In another bowl, cream butter and sugar until smooth
    4. Beat in egg and vanilla, then gradually blend in flour mixture
    5. Roll dough on floured surface and cut with cookie cutters
    6. Bake 8-10 minutes until golden

    Skills We're Building:

    • Safe cutting practice with cookie cutters
    • Shape recognition through different cutter shapes
    • Patience with baking time - good things take time!

    Cookie Success Tips:

    • Chill dough for 30 minutes for easier cutting
    • Use various shaped cookie cutters for extra fun
    • Let cookies cool completely before decorating
    • Make extra for friends and family!
