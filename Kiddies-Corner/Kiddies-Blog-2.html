First Bake

    Magical One-Bowl Brownies

    Ages 4+ - 30 minutes - Minimal mess

    Your First Baking Adventure!

    Our super easy brownie recipe uses just one bowl and simple ingredients. Perfect for little hands to mix and taste-test! This is where the real baking magic begins.

    What You'll Need:

    • 1/2 cup melted butter
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
    • 1/2 cup flour
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    Let's Make Brownies!

    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a small baking pan
    2. In a large bowl, mix melted butter and sugar together
    3. Add eggs and vanilla - mix until smooth
    4. Stir in cocoa powder, flour, and salt
    5. Pour into prepared pan and bake for 20-25 minutes
    6. Let cool before cutting into squares

    Why Kids Love This Recipe:

    • Only 6 ingredients - simple and easy to follow
    • Great for mixing practice - build those little muscles
    • Guaranteed happy faces - who doesn't love brownies?

    Baking Tips for Little Hands:

    • Let kids measure ingredients with your help
    • Use a rubber spatula for easy mixing
    • Create a "taste-test" station for quality control
    • Celebrate their first baking success!
