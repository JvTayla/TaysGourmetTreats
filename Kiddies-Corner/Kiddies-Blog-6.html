    Gingerbread House Adventure

    Ages 7+ - 2 hours - Ultimate baking project

    The Grand Finale Baking Project!

    Our grand finale! Create an entire gingerbread village from scratch. This project brings together all the skills we've learned - baking, decorating, planning, and patience. Get ready for the most epic baking adventure yet!

    Gingerbread House Recipe:

    • 6 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon ginger
    • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 cup butter, softened
    • 1 cup brown sugar
    • 1 1/4 cups molasses
    • 1 egg

    Building Your Gingerbread House:

    1. Make dough and chill for 2 hours
    2. Roll out and cut house templates
    3. Bake pieces until firm
    4. Make royal icing "cement"
    5. Assemble walls and let dry
    6. Add roof and decorate!

    What Makes This Special:

    • Full house construction - architecture meets baking
    • Math and planning skills - measure twice, bake once!
    • Amazing final result - a masterpiece to be proud of

    Gingerbread House Success Tips:

    • Make templates from cardboard first
    • Use cans to support walls while drying
    • Have all decorations ready before assembling
    • Take your time - this is a marathon, not a sprint!
