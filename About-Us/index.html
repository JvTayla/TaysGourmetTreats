About Tay's

    Tay's Gourmet Treats started at the kitchen table with a whisk, a recipe book, and a very patient mum. We believe treats taste better with a story, that's why every cake is hand-decorated and every cookie is made to feel like a hug.

    Our owner, Jordyn Van Aswegen, started this business in 2024 during her 2nd year at university while studying Digital Art and Game Design. She began baking as a way to get back to her roots and to make some extra money as a university student.

    Our small team which is mostly a mix of Jordyn and her unpaid interns (family) loves to mix family recipes with modern flavours and can design everything from classic sugar cookies to bold party centrepieces. We pride ourselves on friendly service, clear communication, and delicious results.

    Manifesto

    At Tay's Gourmet Treats, we believe that every celebration deserves something beautiful, personal, and unforgettable. What began in 2024 as a student's side project — armed with a whisk, a dream, and a love for design — has grown into a heartfelt mission to make life's sweetest moments even sweeter.

    We create Bento Cakes and Themed Cakes that don't just look good — they feel right. Every treat is designed to match the soul and aesthetic of your event, bringing together artistry, taste, and story in every slice.

    We believe in:

    • Quality without compromise: Every ingredient, every detail, and every swirl of frosting reflects our promise of excellence.
    • Creativity with purpose: Each cake tells a story — your story — expressed through color, texture, and design.
    • Empowerment through creation: Tay's isn't just about baking; it's about inspiring others to find joy and pride in what they make, share, and celebrate.
    • Community and connection: Though we began as an at-home bakery, our dream is to grow into a storefront where creativity, kindness, and good taste live side by side.

    We're still small, but our ambitions are big. We see a future where Tay's Gourmet Treats becomes a household name — a place where every person feels welcome, every flavor has a story, and every order carries a touch of love and craftsmanship.

    Because to us, baking isn't just about sugar and flour. It's about creating moments — the kind you can taste, share, and remember.
