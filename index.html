Welcome to Tay’s Gourmet Treats!

What began as a simple passion project in a cozy home kitchen has blossomed into a heartfelt brand built on creativity, quality, and care. Each cake, cupcake, and cookie is designed to bring a touch of joy to your table. All our treats are crafted with love, detail, and a sprinkle of imagination.

From elegant Bento Cakes to playful themed creations, every treat is handcrafted using only the finest ingredients to ensure that each bite tastes just as good as it looks. Our mission is to make every celebration whwether its big or small, a little sweeter, one creation at a time.

Take a moment to explore our story, browse recent orders, and get inspired to order something uniquely you! All our treats are baked with passion, precision, and the personal touch that defines us.
